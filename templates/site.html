    Experiment Guidelines

    Welcome to our economics experiment. In this experiment you will be presented with real introductory economics content, fill a quiz and complete a survey.
    The duration of this experiment is approximately 60 minutes and participation is paid.

    If necessary you may use the calculator app included in your computer

    Content

    You will be presented with a real university introductory economics chapter and you will receive 30 minutes to review this content.

    The Content consists of:

    • Textbook Chapter and Explanations
    • Graphs
    • Optional Practice Questions (Not graded)
    This content will provide everything necessary to do well in the quiz and obtain higher payment.

    If you have any questions you may raise your hand.

    Quiz and Survey

    The quiz will start once the 30 minutes to review the content has passed. The quiz will have 7 questions, which will be based on the content you learn. You will have 10 minutes to complete the quiz, but you can submit the quiz before the 10 minutes have passed.

    The score you obtain in the quiz will determine your final earnings. After finalising the quiz you will complete an anonymous survey.

    Timers

    The Content and Quiz sections of the experiment will show countdown timers displaying the time you have left.
    If the time ends before you submit your quiz it will be automatically submitted for you with your current answers.

    Payment

    The payment amount depends on a show up fee plus a variable amount that depends on your quiz grade.

    • Show Up Fee: $5 dollars (AUD)
    • Fee for finishing the experiment: $15 dollars (AUD)
    • Variable Amount based on quiz grade: Up to a maximum of $5 dollars (AUD)

    If you want to check the instructions at any time just press the icon on the top right

    Title of Project: The Effects of Different Learning Methods on Outcomes

Your name or other personal information is not connected or saved along the rest of your experiment participation.
